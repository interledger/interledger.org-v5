<Paragraph>

The Interledger hackathon brings together developers, designers, and builders to prototype new ideas on top of open payment standards. Teams have a weekend to design, build, and pitch a working project.

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph>

Registration is open to students, professionals, and hobbyists alike — no prior Interledger experience required. Check back here for updates on dates, themes, and how to sign up.

</Paragraph>
