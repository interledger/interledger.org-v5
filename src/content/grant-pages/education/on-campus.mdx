<Paragraph>

On-Campus Financial Education Grant Program is dedicated to empowering innovators who are building the future of the Interledger Protocol (ILP). We offer various grants to support projects that enhance the interoperability, scalability, and real-world applicability of ILP. Our funding supports a diverse range of initiatives, from cutting-edge research to community-driven development. We are committed to fostering a collaborative ecosystem where developers, researchers, and organizations can come together to advance the Interledger Protocol and create a more connected and inclusive global financial system.

</Paragraph>
