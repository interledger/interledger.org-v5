Welcome to **Web Monetization Wrapped**, a look back at the progress we made in 2025 and everything we shipped, fixed, revived, and pushed forward this year.

Turn up the volume. Let’s relive the year. 🎧✨

### 🎼 Top Genre: Strengthening The Ecosystem

![Text reading “More wallet providers supporting Web Monetization, bringing the number to three” on a colorful background.](/img/blog/2025-12-17/wrapped-4.png)

2025 wasn’t theoretical, it was measurable.

- More wallet providers supporting Web Monetization, bringing the number to 3
- More publishers & podcasts experimenting with Web Monetization
- More developers building creative tools and integrations
- More momentum than we've had in years

The needle moved and we moved it.

### 🎵 Top Song: Reviving The Grant Program

![Text reading “More publishers and podcasts experimenting with Web Monetization” displayed over a disco-style background.](/img/blog/2025-12-17/wrapped-3.png)

This was our headline track of 2025.

- Relaunched the Web Monetization Grant Program
- Brought in three publishing platforms that will bring new tools and experiments to their 900+ podcasts, 500,000+ writers, and 200,000 social web users in 2026.
- Strengthened the ecosystem with real, tangible support. $100,000 in financial support , to be exact.

This wasn’t just an initiative, it was a comeback tour.

### 🎥 Your Artist Clip: We Refreshed Our Web Presence

As a thank-you, your Web Monetization browsing experience got a glow up.

- Completely redesigned [webmonetization.org](http://webmonetization.org)
- Modernized the visual identity and restructured the content to make it more accessible, informative, and action-oriented
- Updated and expanded the documentation
- Made information easier to find, clearer to understand, and simpler to act on

Looks better. Works better.

### 💿 Top Albums: We Leveled Up Our Product Offerings

![Graphic presenting a list of product offerings styled as tracks on a CD.](/img/blog/2025-12-17/wrapped-2.png)

Because a standard only moves if we do.

- Expanded [extension](https://github.com/interledger/web-monetization-extension/releases) availability to Safari
- Improved the user experience for the [Publisher Tools](https://webmonetization.org/tools/)
- Shipped new Publisher Tools
- Shipped fixes and docs that unblock users
- Launched a [WordPress Plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/interledger-web-monetization-integration/)
- Resolved bugs and improved overall extension performance
- Developed a prototype for the in-browser implementation.

### ⏱️ Minutes Listened: We Strengthened Community Connections

Not just building tech, building a movement.

- Brought more publishers, partners, and developers into the conversation
- Received impactful code contributions from community developers who stepped up for Hacktoberfest.
- Supported community events and working groups
- Listened, iterated, and responded to ecosystem needs and community feedback.

More voices, more momentum.
