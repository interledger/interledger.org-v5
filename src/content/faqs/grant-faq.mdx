        question: 'What is the Interledger Foundation?'
        answer: 'The Interledger Foundation is a private operating foundation incorporated in California, United States. We steward open payment technologies, including the Interledger Protocol, the Open Payments API, and the Web Monetization technology standard, and make grants to advance access to digital financial services globally.  We are not a financial institution of any kind, we do not operate a payment network, nor do we earn revenue from payments. We are not a cryptocurrency, do not have a token, and our technology does not use blockchain. Our role is to support the development, adoption, and governance of open standards that enable interoperable, inclusive payment infrastructure.'
