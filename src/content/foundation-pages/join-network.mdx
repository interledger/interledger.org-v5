## What is the Interledger Network?

The Interledger Network is an open inter-networking system for payment networks. This enables money to move between disconnected financial systems without requiring custom integrations or standardization of ledgers.

At its core, the Interledger Protocol (ILP) is a messaging and real-time clearing layer. You retain full control over your accounts, balances, compliance processes, and the timing and method of settlement.

## Why Join the Network?

- Reduce operational overhead by replacing bespoke integrations with standardized protocols.
- Expand across currencies and countries, using your existing infrastructure.
- Enable secure third-party access, aligned with Open Banking and Open Finance trends. By empowering customers to pay directly from their accounts, you also reduce reliance on third party services and stay at the center of the transaction flow.
- Open new revenue streams for your users like digital tipping, streaming payments, or pay-per-use models.

## Rafiki: Your Gateway to Interledger

Rafiki is a production-ready, open-source implementation of the Interledger protocol suite. It is developed for regulated Account Servicing Entities (ASEs) such as banks, digital wallets, mobile money providers, clearing houses, and so on.

Integrate Rafiki with your core systems to become Interledger-enabled—and unlock seamless, secure, real-time payments across the network, as well as:

- High-throughput transaction processing
- Smart, real-time routing across connected peers
- Open Payments APIs to support delegated access and secure payment initiation
- Multi-tenancy support, enabling a single Rafiki instance to service multiple ASEs
- Settlement remains on your terms, using existing payment rails

## How to Join?

Join the Interledger Network by integrating Rafiki and connecting with one other provider on the network.
