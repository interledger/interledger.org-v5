## Unlock the Internet of Opportunity with Open Payments

Join us in Mexico City on November 8 and 9 for our annual 24-hour Interledger Hackathon! Dive into the world of Open Payments and develop innovative solutions using the Interledger Open Payments API.

## Win amazing prizes!

Your ideas can make the internet more open and inclusive for everyone. Build with purpose — and earn prizes that celebrate real-world impact.

We’re also offering Platzi scholarships! Enjoy 60 days of full access to one of Latin America’s leading online learning platforms. To apply, simply complete the free Open Payments with Interledger course and pass the final exam. For more details, please read the notes at the bottom of the hackathon resources page.

**We're looking for creative solutions that:**

- Simplify cross-border and cross-currency transactions: Develop tools that make international and multi-currency payments effortless and accessible.
- Enhance financial inclusion: Create applications that provide access to essential financial services.
- Automate payments and financial workflows: Build solutions to streamline payment processes, reduce friction, and increase efficiency.
