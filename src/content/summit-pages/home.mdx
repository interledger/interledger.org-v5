## Connecting Global Financial Infrastructure

Interledger Summit brings together the people, platforms, and policies driving the future of financial interoperability. As payment systems continue to evolve, Interledger enables the connective layer that allows money to move as freely, openly, and instantly as information.

At the heart of this work is the Interledger Protocol, a global financial network where anyone, anywhere, can access and benefit from inclusive, borderless payments, regardless of geography, income, or infrastructure.
