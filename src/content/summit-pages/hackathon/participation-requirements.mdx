## 2025 Participation requirements

- You must be 18 or older to participate.
- Teams of 1 to 4 participants can enter.
- This hackathon will take place in English and Spanish.
- Bring your laptop along. We will not provide equipment.
- You need to attend both days and demo your solution.
- This is an in-person hackathon; no remote participation is possible.
- There are limited spaces available, so look out for your confirmation of a secured spot.
- If you are offered a place, you must accept it within 3 working days, or it may be offered to someone else.
- All code needs to be developed within the 24 hours during the hackathon.
- We do not pay for transportation and accommodations.
- Please review our Terms & Conditions before applying.
