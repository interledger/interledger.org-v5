## Our mission

The Interledger Foundation advances the Interledger Protocol, a technology for routing payments across different networks. We focus on open standards, financial inclusion, and building a global ecosystem that makes value move as freely as information.

## What we do

- Maintain and promote open standards for interoperable payments.
- Fund research, pilots, and community initiatives that broaden access.
- Support developers, partners, and educators building on Interledger.

## Our values

- Openness and interoperability.
- Global inclusion and resilience.
- Collaboration with the community.
