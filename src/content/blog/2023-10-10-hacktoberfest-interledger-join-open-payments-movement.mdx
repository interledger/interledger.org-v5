## What is Hacktoberfest?

[Hacktoberfest](https://hacktoberfest.com/) is a month-long celebration of Open Source that encourages people to contribute to open source throughout October. This year marks the 10th edition of Hacktoberfest. Every year, thousands of contributors participate in this festival to contribute to the projects they love and use, learn and enhance their skills to advance their careers, get mentorship, and feedback from industry experts. For organizations that believe in giving back to the community, Hacktoberfest is a fantastic platform.

## Few repositories that are participating in Hacktoberfest

## Rafiki

Rafiki is open-source software that allows an Account Servicing Entity to enable Interledger functionality on its users' accounts.

This includes


- sending and receiving payments (via SPSP and Open Payments)
- allowing third-party access to initiate payments and view transaction data (via Open Payments)

**Repo link** - [https://github.com/interledger/rafiki](https://github.com/interledger/rafiki)

**Contribution guide** - [https://github.com/interledger/rafiki/blob/main/.github/contributing.md](https://github.com/interledger/rafiki/blob/main/.github/contributing.md)

## Testnet

Testnet is an open-source test application for Rafiki. In other words, a Rafiki Playground. It wants to use all of the functionalities of Rafiki and put its advantages of to the real test.

Testnet is made up (or will be made up in the near future) of several components, including a wallet application, a bank application, and an e-commerce application.

**Repo link** - [https://github.com/interledger/testnet](https://github.com/interledger/testnet)

**Contribution guide** - [https://github.com/interledger/testnet/blob/main/.github/contributing.md](https://github.com/interledger/testnet/blob/main/.github/contributing.md)
