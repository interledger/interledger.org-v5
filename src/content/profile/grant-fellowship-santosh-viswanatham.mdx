## Ambassador Project

Santosh worked on strengthening the Web Monetization ecosystem by improving documentation, migrating libraries to the latest specification, launching community guides, and building a contributor recognition program. Through global talks, Hacktoberfest engagement, and community collaboration, he helped expand awareness and participation in open, inclusive financial infrastructure.

Through **global talks across Asia and Europe, engaging more than 1,300** contributors during Hacktoberfest, **and launching initiatives like** Get Involved guides and the Open Badges program, **Santosh helped strengthen both the developer experience and global community participation**.

## What Inspired His Work

Coming from India, Santosh witnessed firsthand how digital and financially inclusive systems can transform lives within a single decade. The rapid adoption of digital payments and inclusive financial infrastructure has empowered millions, bringing small businesses online, enabling instant transactions, and giving individuals access to formal financial systems for the first time.

## Vision for the Future

Santosh envisions a world where financial inclusion is seamlessly built into the web.

_"I envision a future where financial inclusion is naturally embedded into the web, allowing anyone, anywhere, to seamlessly send, receive, and earn money online without unnecessary barriers."_
